Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions Vancouver Island homeowners ask us most.

What areas do you serve?
We're locally owned and operated in Nanaimo and serve homes from Duncan to Qualicum Beach, including Lantzville, Parksville, Chemainus and the surrounding mid-Vancouver Island communities.
How often should gutters be cleaned?
For most homes on Vancouver Island we recommend cleaning gutters 1–2 times a year depending on how many trees surround your property, to prevent overflow damage to your roof, fascia, siding and foundation.
Do you pressure wash roofs?
No. We never pressure wash roofs — it can be extremely damaging. We remove moss and debris with non-abrasive nylon brushes and apply a professional-grade, biodegradable moss-prevention treatment that lasts 1–3 years.
Are your cleaning products safe?
Yes. Our soft washing and cleaning solutions are biodegradable, eco-friendly and pet-friendly, and are gentle on vinyl siding and sensitive surfaces such as asphalt shingles.
