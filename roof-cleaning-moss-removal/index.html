Roof Cleaning & Moss Removal in Nanaimo

Vancouver Island's damp climate is hard on roofs. We safely remove moss, lichen and buildup — without ever pressure washing — to protect your roof and keep it looking its best.

Why roof cleaning matters

Moss holds moisture against your shingles and works its way under them, lifting and cracking them over time. Cleaning your roof and removing moss protects one of the most expensive parts of your home. The benefits add up quickly:

  • Prevent leaks, water damage and rot
  • Increase curb appeal and the potential value of your home
  • Extend the life of your roof
  • Increase energy efficiency

Our safe, non-abrasive process

We remove all debris, moss and leaves using non-abrasive nylon brushes. We NEVER pressure wash roofs — this can be extremely damaging. Once the roof is clean, we apply a professional-grade, biodegradable moss-prevention solution that keeps moss from coming back for 1–3 years depending on your area.

What's included

  • All debris, moss and leaves removed
  • Biodegradable moss-preventative treatment
  • Branch trimming where needed to reduce regrowth
  • Eco-friendly and non-abrasive methods
  • Before & after pictures
