Holiday Light Installation in Nanaimo

Make your home shine this season — without the ladders. We design, install and take down holiday lighting for homes and businesses across Vancouver Island.

Brighten the season, skip the ladder

Hanging holiday lights means hours on a ladder in cold, wet weather. Let our insured local crew handle it for you — safely and beautifully. We work with you to plan a look you'll love, then take care of the rest from start to finish.

What we offer

  • Custom design to suit your home or business
  • Safe, professional installation by insured crews
  • Mid-season service if anything needs attention
  • Takedown after the season — and tidy storage

Residential & commercial

Whether it's a cozy rooflines-and-railings display for your home or a polished look for your storefront, we bring the same care and attention to detail we put into every B&B Exteriors job.
