Why Moss Is A Problem On Vancouver Island

Moss and algae hold moisture extremely well, and trap that moisture against your roof. This can lead to water damage and even leaks. Here on Vancouver Island, moss buildup is one of the most common problems homeowners face thanks to our damp, west-coast climate. Left untreated, moss growth can reduce the lifespan of a shingle roof by up to 40% — making moss removal one of the most valuable maintenance services for protecting your home.

The benefits of professional roof cleaning

  • Prevent leaks, water damage and rot
  • Increase curb appeal and potentially the value of your home
  • Extend the life of your roof
  • Increase energy efficiency
