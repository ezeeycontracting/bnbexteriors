Window Cleaning in Nanaimo

Streak-free, crystal-clear windows inside and out — letting more natural light in and making your whole home or business shine.

Crystal-clear results, every time

Clean windows change the whole feel of a space — more natural light comes in, and everything looks brighter and better cared for. We clean windows for both homes and businesses across the mid-island.

What you can expect

  • Residential & commercial window cleaning
  • Maximized natural light
  • Bio-degradable and eco-friendly solutions
  • Spotless results for a crisp, clear view

Our method

Using squeegees, wipe-off cloths and the proper cleaning solution, we start at the top and follow a reverse-S pattern down the windows to the corners — leaving no drips and no streaks. It's a simple, proven technique that delivers a flawless finish.

How often should windows be cleaned?

Generally you should have your windows cleaned twice a year. The most common times for window cleaning are in the spring and when getting ready to sell your home.
