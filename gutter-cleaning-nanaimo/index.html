Protect Your Home From Water Damage

Gutter cleaning plays an essential role when it comes to maintaining your home, especially here on Vancouver Island where we have an abundance of leaves, needles and rain. When gutters clog and water overflows, it can damage your roof, fascia, siding and even your foundation. Here at B&B Exteriors we treat your home like the major investment it is, and keeping your gutters clear is one of the simplest ways to protect it.

Why regular gutter cleaning matters

  • Prevents damage to your roof, fascia, siding and foundation caused by overflow
  • Regular maintenance extends the lifespan of your gutters
  • Increases curb appeal and keeps your home looking its best

What's Included In Our Gutter Cleaning Service

We don't take shortcuts. Every gutter clean is done by hand so the work is thorough and the results last longer between cleans.

  • All leaves, needles and debris are cleared from the roof
  • Gutters are hand cleaned to ensure quality of work and extend time between cleans
  • Free maintenance check and all downspouts water tested
  • Before and after photos to guarantee your satisfaction

How Often Should You Clean Your Gutters?

Gutter cleaning should be done at least 1–2 times a year depending on your area and how many trees surround your home. Properties under heavy tree cover often need a clean in both spring and fall. That's why you call B&B Exteriors for professional, efficient and high-quality cleanings — so you never have to climb a ladder or worry about a blocked downspout again.

Pairing your gutter clean with our roof cleaning & moss removal or a full soft wash is a great way to refresh and protect the whole exterior of your home in one visit.
