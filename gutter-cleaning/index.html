Gutter Cleaning in Nanaimo

Clogged gutters can't move water — and water that overflows finds your roof, fascia, siding and foundation. We hand-clean gutters across Vancouver Island so your home stays protected.

Protect your home from water damage

Your gutters have one job: move rainwater safely away from your home. When they fill with leaves, needles and debris, water overflows and can cause expensive damage. Regular gutter cleaning prevents damage to your roof, fascia, siding and foundation caused by overflow.

Hand-cleaned for a quality result

We hand-clean your gutters to ensure the quality of work and to extend the time between cleans. All of our technicians are highly trained in all things gutters — so we know our way around them inside and out, on every style of home from bungalows to multi-storey houses.

What's included

  • Removal of all leaves, needles, debris and blockages
  • Hand cleaning to protect your gutters and extend time between cleans
  • A check that water flows freely toward the downspouts
  • Tidy-up so your property is left clean and clear

How often should gutters be cleaned?

Gutter cleaning should be done at least 1–2 times a year depending on your area and how many trees surround your property. Homes shaded by tall evergreens on Vancouver Island often need a clean before and after the wet season.
