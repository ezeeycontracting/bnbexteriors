Let Us Maintain Your Biggest Investment

You spent a long time working towards the investment that is your home, and you could spend the rest of your life maintaining it. Let us take care of that for you. Vancouver Island's rainy climate creates all kinds of issues for your siding — from dirt, lichen and mildew, to man-made discoloration and salt stains.

Here's the important part: pressure washing does almost nothing against these issues, and can even damage your siding. Soft washing uses a low-pressure cleaning solution that breaks down and removes the growth and grime at the source, so your home gets a deep, lasting clean without any risk of damage.

What soft washing takes care of

  • Suitable for vinyl siding and sensitive surfaces like asphalt shingles
  • Targets moss, algae, lichen, bacteria, mildew, stains and discoloration
  • Bio-degradable, eco-friendly and pet friendly cleaning solution
