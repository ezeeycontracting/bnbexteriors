Soft Washing in Nanaimo

A gentle, low-pressure clean that lifts dirt, algae and grime from siding and surfaces — even in the hardest-to-reach places — without causing damage.

A gentler way to clean your home

Soft washing uses a low-pressure cleaning solution that gets rid of all that nasty stuff even in the hardest to reach places. Vancouver Island's weather throws a lot at your home — everything from dirt, lichen and mildew to man-made discoloration and salt stains. Soft washing tackles it all without the risk that high-pressure washing brings.

What soft washing removes

  • Moss, algae and lichen
  • Bacteria and mildew
  • Stains and unnatural discoloration
  • Dirt, grime and salt buildup

Safe for your surfaces

Our solutions are biodegradable, eco-friendly and pet-friendly. The low-pressure approach protects vinyl siding and sensitive surfaces such as asphalt shingles, so your home gets clean without the damage that comes from blasting it with high pressure.

Full house washing

A full house wash takes care of your siding, windows and gutter facings all in one go — leaving the whole exterior looking refreshed and cared for.
